The Earth is comparable to our mother, as is widely known. It provides us with all the necessities for a happy life, nourishing us. Our use of the earth's resources depletes them gradually over time. And our use of these resources is outpacing their resupply. That leads to our own demise, unfortunately.

In today's world, individuals lament the oppressive heat, respiratory issues, and general lack of health. It is time to stop and reflect on the reason for this entire situation.


The greenhouse effect of gases is accelerating global warming. Galloping population growth, unmanaged industrialisation, mining, and careless and poor urban planning are the causes of environmental degradation.


Deforestation to make more land available for commercial use has increased the problem by indiscriminately cutting down trees. toxic smoke is released.
